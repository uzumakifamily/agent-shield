Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 May 2026 · Applies to agentshield.allkinz.com

1. Who We Are

AgentShield is a runtime control layer for AI agents, operated by Allkinz (sole trader). Our registered contact email is privacy@agentshield.allkinz.com. When we say "we", "our", or "AgentShield", we mean Allkinz operating this service.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to operate the service. Specifically:

  • Account data — your email address, hashed password (never stored in plain text), and workspace name when you sign up.
  • Agent action metadata — action type, risk score, verdict (ALLOW / PAUSE / BLOCK), timestamp, and agent identifier sent through the API. We do not store raw payloads from your agents by default.
  • Usage events — anonymised page views and feature interaction counts via Plausible Analytics (no cookies, no cross-site tracking, GDPR-compliant by default).
  • IP address — logged temporarily for rate limiting and abuse prevention. Not stored beyond 24 hours or linked to your account profile.
  • Telegram chat ID — only if you connect a Telegram bot for alerts. Stored in your workspace settings, used only to deliver alerts you explicitly requested.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and operate your AgentShield account.
  • To deliver the core service: evaluating agent actions, logging decisions, sending approval alerts.
  • To send transactional emails (OTP verification codes, approval notifications). We do not send marketing emails.
  • To monitor service health and debug issues.

We do not use your data to train AI models, sell to third parties, or profile you for advertising.

4. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored on Railway.app infrastructure (EU/US regions). Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2 with SHA-256 (100,000 iterations) and a random salt — they are never readable by us.

API keys are generated with cryptographic randomness and stored in hashed form. Your workspace is logically isolated from other workspaces at the database level.

Self-hosted instances: if you run the open-source version yourself, all data stays entirely on your own infrastructure. We have no access to it.

5. Data Retention

  • Agent action logs are retained for 90 days on the managed cloud tier, then automatically deleted.
  • Account data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • On account deletion, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

6. Third-Party Services

We use a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Railway.app — hosting and database infrastructure.
  • Plausible Analytics — cookieless, GDPR-compliant analytics. No personal data shared.
  • Brevo (Sendinblue) — transactional email delivery (OTP codes only). Your email address is shared with Brevo to deliver emails you request.
  • Google OAuth — if you choose to sign in with Google. We receive only your email address and display name from Google.

We do not use advertising networks, analytics with tracking pixels, or any service that sets third-party cookies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Export your audit log data (available from the dashboard).
  • Object to any processing beyond what is necessary to deliver the service.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@agentshield.allkinz.com. We will respond within 5 business days.

8. Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising. The service uses sessionStorage to hold your authentication token locally in your browser. This is cleared when you close the browser tab and is never sent to third parties.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you by email. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy: privacy@agentshield.allkinz.com

General support: manager@allkinz.com
