Setup in minutes

How it works

Three steps from zero to a fully protected agent.

1

Install & wrap

npm i agent-shield-core. Wrap your agent function in one line. Risk scoring starts immediately — no config required.

2

Set your thresholds

Define what risk level triggers a block, an alert, or a pass-through. Default config works out of the box for most agents.

3

Receive alerts, review & approve

High-risk actions fire a Telegram alert. Open the dashboard, review the full context, and click approve or deny.

What you get

Everything you need to trust your agents

Risk Scoring Engine

Every action gets a 0–1.0 score based on action type, source, blast radius, and PII presence. Fully configurable thresholds per agent.

Full Trace Recording

Every action is logged with full context — payload, timestamp, risk score, source, and the decision made. Immutable audit trail.

Instant Telegram Alerts

High-risk actions fire a Telegram message immediately with full action details and inline approve / deny buttons.

Policy Rules Engine

Define custom rules: "block all shell_execute from web_content" or "always require approval for payments > $100." No code needed.

Emergency Kill Switch

One button in the dashboard halts all agent activity instantly. Approval queue drains safely with zero partial-state corruption.

Audit & Compliance

SOC-2 ready audit trail. Export CSV. Every decision, every reviewer, every timestamp — exactly what compliance teams need.
